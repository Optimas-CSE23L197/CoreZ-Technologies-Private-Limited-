Privacy
Policy.

How RESQID collects, uses, and protects the personal data of students, parents, and schools.

Upd: March 2026  ·  DPDPA 2023 Compliant
Our core commitment to you

RESQID is built entirely on trust. We collect only the minimum data necessary to keep students safe in emergencies and we will never sell, rent, or misuse your data under any circumstances.

Section 01
1

Who We Are

RESQID (accessible at getresqid.in) is a student safety platform that provides QR-based emergency identity cards for school students across India. We are based in India and comply with applicable Indian data protection laws including the Information Technology Act, 2000, the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, and the IT (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures) Rules, 2011.

The platform was developed by a team of final-year B.Tech students from CEMK with the goal of enabling faster emergency response for children.

Section 02
2

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the emergency profile service. Here is exactly what we store:

Data Category What It Includes Who Provides It
Student Profile Name, date of birth, photo, blood group, allergies, medical conditions, school name, class Parent / School Admin
Emergency Contacts Parent/guardian name, phone number(s), relationship to student Parent / School Admin
Scan Location GPS coordinates recorded at the moment a QR card is scanned Automatically captured
Account Data Email address, encrypted password of the registering user Registering user
Device & Usage Device type, browser, IP address, timestamps of scans and logins Automatically captured
Section 03
3

How We Use Your Information

  • Display emergency medical information when a student's QR card is scanned by a rescuer or passerby
  • Send real-time push notifications to parents/guardians the instant a QR scan event occurs
  • Show the scan location to the registered parent/guardian for immediate student safety response
  • Enable a direct emergency call from the scan page to the registered parent without exposing the phone number to the scanner
  • Maintain and continuously improve the RESQID platform and its emergency features
  • Send service-related communications and critical safety notices to registered users
  • Detect and alert on anomalous scan patterns that may indicate risk
Section 04
4

Who Can See Student Data

When a QR code is scanned by any person (rescuer, passerby, school staff), they will see:

  • Student's first name and photo (if uploaded by the parent)
  • Blood group and key medical information such as allergies and conditions
  • An emergency call button this initiates a direct call to the parent without ever showing the phone number to the scanner

Detailed scan logs, location history, and full contact details are accessible only to the registered parent/guardian account. School administrators can only view profiles of students enrolled under their institution.

Section 05
5

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data with any third party. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Registered school administrators only for students explicitly enrolled under their institution
  • Cloud infrastructure providers solely for operating the platform, under strict data processing agreements
  • Government or law enforcement only when required by a valid court order or applicable Indian law

We do not share data with advertisers. No ad networks have access to any RESQID user data.

Section 06
6

Data Retention

  • Student profiles: Retained while the account is active; permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • QR scan logs & location data: Retained for 12 months from each scan date, then automatically purged
  • Account login data: Retained until account deletion
  • System security logs: Retained for 6 months for security auditing purposes
Section 07
7

Your Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the following rights regarding your data:

Access data we hold about you or your child

Request correction of inaccurate information

Delete your account and all associated data

Withdraw consent at any time (deactivates QR card)

Receive a portable copy of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at: privacy@getresqid.in

Section 08
8

Children's Privacy

RESQID is specifically designed to protect children's safety. Student data is always registered by parents or authorised school administrators never by children themselves. We do not knowingly collect data directly from individuals under 18 years of age. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately at privacy@getresqid.in.

Section 09
9

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the platform evolves. Registered users will be notified of significant changes via email or in-app notification before the change takes effect. The date at the top of this page always reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions about your privacy?

Our team responds to all privacy queries promptly. You can also request data access or deletion via email.
